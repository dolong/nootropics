Bacopa Monnieri

Summary:

Bacopa Monnieri is an herb which has been used in Ayurvedic medicine for centuries. It's currently one of the most well tested nootropics. A 2012 review of 6 trials on Bacopa Monnieri noted large and statistically significant memory enhancing effects.[27][28] Bacopa's primary mechanism of action is still unclear, it seems be an anti-oxidant, a weak acetylcholinesterase inhibitor, a cerebral blood flow activator and neurotransmitter modulator.[29] In animal models, Bacopa Monnieri seems to increase cortical concentrations of acetylcholine, dopamine and serotonin.[197] Bacopa Monnieri is likely to be one of the safest and most effective memory enhancers.

Standard Dose:

300mg assuming 50% bacosides once in the morning or 750mg assuming 20% bacosides

(Note: Bacopa Monnieri's memory enhancing effects appear with long-term continuous use, 8 weeks is a good minimum trial period)
